Raise Collateral Transfer within 10-15 working days for your Small Medium Enterprise. Loans from €2m to €5m are available from the minimum term of 12 months or the maximum of 24 months. Loans are available at 1.3% per month, based on a minimum of a 12-month contract.

Collateral Transfer Executive

Collateral Transfer can be applied for on a minimum term of 12 months or maximum term of 24 months, with the option of between £5-10m loans. The loans are available at a rate of 1.1% per months, based on a 12-month contract. The turnaround for the loan is between 10-15 working days.

Collateral Transfer Corporate

Corporate Collateral Transfer is a means of flexible financing where assets or existing loans or credit facilities are restricted and are unable to be used to extend or open new lines of credit. Collateral Transfer allows the borrower access to assets (collateral) to use as security for credit or loans.
